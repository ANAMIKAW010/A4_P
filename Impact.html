The Four Pillars of Our Footprint

Cultural Resonance

We actively promote indigenous arts by integrating local village tours into 80% of our itineraries, ensuring ancient traditions are celebrated, funded, and passed down to the next generation.

Historic Preservation

A portion of every booking goes directly to the ASI (Archaeological Survey of India) and local heritage trusts to fund the restoration of lesser-known stepwells, forts, and ruins.

Ecological Balance

We partner with zero-waste eco-lodges in the Himalayas and the Western Ghats. Our "Leave No Trace" mandate has successfully removed 5,000+ kgs of plastic from popular trekking routes.

Economic Empowerment

By prioritizing homestays and community-owned transport, 65% of your travel spend stays directly within the local rural economy, bypassing massive corporate hotel chains.

The Canvas Promise: 5% For The Future

We believe travel is a privilege. That is why 5% of every tour package profit is directly deposited into our partnered NGO network, funding education and digital literacy for underprivileged children in rural Rajasthan and Bihar.
