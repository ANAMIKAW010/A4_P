Top 10 Destinations in India

From the peaks of the Himalayas to the backwaters of the South.

#1

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

HeritageArchitecture

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a world wonder and symbol of eternal love. Agra offers a deep dive into Mughal architecture, featuring majestic forts and ancient tombs.

#2

Jaipur, Rajasthan

RoyaltyCulture

Known as the 'Pink City', Jaipur is a vibrant showcase of Rajputana grandeur. Explore the massive Amber Fort, intricate City Palace, and bustling colorful bazaars.

#3

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

SpiritualHistory

One of the oldest living cities in the world. Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony on the river ghats and experience the profound spiritual heartbeat of India.

#4

Alleppey, Kerala

NatureRelaxation

Experience 'God's Own Country' by drifting through a tranquil labyrinth of palm-fringed backwaters on a traditional wooden houseboat.

#5

Goa

BeachesNightlife

A coastal paradise blending pristine beaches, historic Portuguese architecture, vibrant nightlife, and a laid-back lifestyle unmatched anywhere else.

#6

Ladakh

AdventureLandscapes

A high-altitude desert known for its stark, breathtaking mountains, crystal-clear azure lakes like Pangong Tso, and ancient Buddhist monasteries.

#7

Hampi, Karnataka

Ancient RuinsHistory

A UNESCO World Heritage site scattered with captivating boulders and the majestic ruins of the ancient Vijayanagara Empire.

#8

Darjeeling, West Bengal

MountainsTea Estates

Nestled in the Himalayas, this charming hill station is famous worldwide for its aromatic tea plantations and the historic 'Toy Train'.

#9

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

YogaAdventure

The Yoga Capital of the World. Set against the backdrop of the Himalayas, it offers both profound spiritual retreats and thrilling river rafting.

#10

Kaziranga, Assam

WildlifeSafaris

A lush biodiversity hotspot and the proud home to the world's largest population of the magnificent one-horned rhinoceros.
