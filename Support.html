Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Most international travelers require an e-Tourist Visa. Apply online through the official government portal prior to arrival.

Are your itineraries customizable?

Yes! Our Smart Planner generates a baseline itinerary you can adjust, swap destinations on, and save to your profile.

How do you ensure ethical tourism?

We partner with verified local guides, promote cultural circuits, and track community impact on our Impact Dashboard.

Who do I contact in an emergency?

Dial 112 for immediate help in India. For itinerary emergencies, use the 24/7 Live Chat in your Account Dashboard.

Travel Advisory Details

Health & Safety Guidelines

Carry basic medications including antacids and ORS. Drink only sealed bottled water. Consult your doctor about vaccinations (Hepatitis A, Typhoid) at least 4–6 weeks before travel.

Currency & Payments

India uses the Indian Rupee (INR). UPI, credit cards, and ATMs are widely available in cities. Carry cash for rural areas. Avoid unauthorized money exchangers.

Transport Tips

Use registered taxi services (Ola, Uber) in cities. Book trains via the IRCTC website. Domestic flights connect major cities efficiently. Always verify auto-rickshaw meters.
