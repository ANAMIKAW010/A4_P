Traveller Essentials

Everything you need to know before you land

Visas & Entry

Most travelers require an e-Tourist Visa to enter India. Ensure your passport has at least 6 months validity.

Official Visa Portal

Weather & Seasons

Peak season is Oct–Mar. Monsoons arrive in June. Pack light cottons for the plains and heavy woolens for the North.

Ethics & Safety

Respect local traditions: Remove shoes at temples and dress modestly. Drink only bottled water and use registered transport.

Emergency: 112 (All-in-one helpline)
