Travel & Visa Updates

The latest news to make your journey to India smoother than ever.

E-Visa Facility Expanded

The Indian government has officially expanded the quick electronic visa (E-Visa) facility to citizens of over 170 countries, drastically reducing processing times for tourists and business travelers.

New Vande Bharat Express Routes

India's semi-high-speed railway network continues to grow, with new dedicated routes instantly connecting major tourist hubs like Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and Varanasi in record time.

DigiYatra Rolls Out Nationwide

Major international airports across India have implemented 'DigiYatra', a seamless, paperless facial recognition system allowing travelers to clear security and boarding completely hands-free.
